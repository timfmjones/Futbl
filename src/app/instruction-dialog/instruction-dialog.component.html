How to play

A random player is chosen, guess players to discover who the mystery player is

If the player you guessed plays in the same position as the mystery player, the position will appear green. If the guessed player plays in a similar position, it will be yellow.

If the player you guessed plays in the same league as the mystery player, the team will be yellow. If they are on the same team, the team will be green.

If the player you guessed is from the same continent as the mystery player, the nation will be yellow. If they are from the same country, the country will be green.
